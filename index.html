Company Profile

Eric Murray

Eric runs the office and is in charge of all estimating and marketing for Murray Bros. He is the recipient of the INC500 award in 2004 & 2005 for a web conferencing company he started in 1999. Eric is actively involved in customer support to make sure all of our customers have a positive experience dealing with our company. Feel free to contact Eric to set up a meeting to discuss any upcoming projects you may have.

Joe Murray

Joe runs the field operations for Murray Bros. and handles all scheduling and day to day operations. He has foremen that report directly to him with any questions or concerns with any given job we may be working on. Joe has been pouring foundations for the better part of 25 years and still out works even our best employees. He is there to oversee all aspects of your job from the laying out to setting the grades. His meticulous eye for detail ensures our customers of a quality foundation they can build their dream home on!
